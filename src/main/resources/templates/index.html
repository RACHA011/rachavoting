Voting Process

Follow these steps to participate in elections

1. Registration

Register as a voter before the registration deadline. No registrations are accepted during voting periods.

2. Wait for Election

After successful registration, wait for the official voting period to begin.

3. Cast Your Vote

During the voting period, log in and submit your ballot. Each voter can only vote once per election.
