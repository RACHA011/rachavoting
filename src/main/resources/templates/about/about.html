About RachaVoting

RachaVoting is dedicated to making the democratic process more accessible, transparent, and secure for all citizens.

Our Mission

At RachaVoting, we believe that participation in the democratic process should be accessible to every eligible citizen. Our mission is to provide a secure, user-friendly platform that makes voting convenient while maintaining the integrity of the electoral process.

We are committed to transparency, security, and inclusivity in all aspects of our service, ensuring that every voice can be heard in our democracy.

Transparency

Frequently Asked Questions

How does online voting work?

RachaVoting provides a secure platform where eligible voters can access their ballot, make their selections, and submit their vote. The system verifies the voter's identity while maintaining the anonymity of their selections.

Is online voting secure?

Yes. RachaVoting employs multiple layers of security, including encryption, authentication protocols, and regular security audits to ensure the integrity of the voting process and protect voter data.

How do you prevent fraud?

Our system includes multiple verification steps to confirm voter identity, prevents duplicate voting, and maintains a secure audit trail of all voting activity while preserving voter anonymity.

What if I need assistance while voting?

RachaVoting provides comprehensive help resources, including step-by-step guides and a support team available via phone, email, or chat during voting periods.
