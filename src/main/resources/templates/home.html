How the Voting Process Works

A step-by-step guide to casting your vote online

  1. 1

    Register for Voting

    South African citizens must register for voting during the registration period before the deadline. Upon successful registration, you will receive a confirmation message.

  2. 2

    Log In to the Portal

    During the active voting period, log in to your account using your credentials.

  3. 3

    Access Active Elections

    View the list of current elections you are eligible to participate in.

  4. 4

    Cast Your Vote

    Select your preferred candidate or party and confirm your selection during the voting period.

  5. 5

    Receive Confirmation

    After casting your vote, you will receive a confirmation message verifying that you have successfully voted

Voting Requirements

Eligibility criteria and necessary documents

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must be a registered member of a recognized political party
  • Must be at least 18 years of age
  • Must be a South African citizen
  • Must have a valid party membership ID

Required Documents

  • South African ID number
  • Party membership identification number
  • Valid email address for verification
  • Mobile phone number for two-factor authentication

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the online voting system.

Is online voting secure?

Yes, our system uses end-to-end encryption, blockchain technology, and multiple verification steps to ensure secure voting. Your vote is private and cannot be traced back to you.

Who is eligible to vote?

All South African citizens aged 18 and over are eligible to vote

Can I change my vote after submitting?

No, once your vote is submitted, it cannot be changed. Make sure to review your selection carefully before confirming your vote.
